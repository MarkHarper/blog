Catching Up: Part One

27 July 2015

Wow, it's been awhile.

So, what have I been up to? In short, finishing up with the program at TIY. My last post covered week eight of the program. Since then, the last week of lectures ended and we spent three weeks working our tails off to make some killer final projects.

Here's a brief recap of the last lecture week (that was week 9 by the way)

  • Similarities and Differences between Python and Javascript
  • A python-front end collaboration for Mon & Tues: Activities
  • One final overview of design conventions. Man oh man! It was fun to tear down some out of date websites and talk about ways to improve them. Our instructor wanted to make sure we avoided some serious pitfalls going into final projects.

After our last lectures, we jumped right into pitching our final projects. As I did not have a pitch to make, I had the pleasure of choosing one project out of so many great ideas. Where did I land? That's for next time.

Mark Harper
